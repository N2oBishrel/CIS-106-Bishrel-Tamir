This program prompts the user for the name of their dog and its age in human years. 
It then calculates and displays the age of their dog in dog years, 
based on the popular myth that one human year equals seven dog years. 

Input:

    Display prompt
    Get dog's name

    Display prompt
    Get age (human years)

    Display prompt 
    Get Month
    
    Display prompt
    Get choice
Process:

    Calculate age in dog years (years * 7)

    
Output:

    Display dog's name and age in dog years.
   
    Enter dog`s month. 
    
    Enter Y to convert dogs age to humans or N to get dogs life stage.
